Bpesa Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-07-01
Last Updated: 2025-08-01

Overview

Bpesa (“we,” “our,” “us”) is a decentralized digital payments application for emerging markets. We respect your privacy and are transparent about how we handle information.

This Privacy Policy explains our practices for the Bpesa mobile app, website, and related services (“Services”). By using the Services, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Bpesa does not collect, store, or share personal information, device identifiers, technical telemetry, biometric data, or analytics about you.

The only data inherently involved when using Bpesa relates to transactions (e.g., transfers), which are publicly available by design on their respective networks. We do not link these to your identity.

2. How We Use Data

Because we do not collect personal or device data, we do not use information for advertising, analytics, tracking, or profiling. The app facilitates decentralized payments and interacts with blockchain networks only.

3. Third-Party Services

Bpesa does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or data collection tools. If you follow links to external sites or use external services, their own privacy policies apply.

4. Data Security

We do not collect or store personal data on our servers. Transactions occur over decentralized infrastructure. Always follow best practices to secure your device and any wallet credentials you manage.

5. Children’s Privacy

Bpesa is intended for individuals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly offer services to, or collect information from, children under 18.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect technical or legal changes. When we do, we will revise the “Last Updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide notice within the app or on our website.
